Solution for colorblind

EnChroma lenses were developed in the early 2000's by glass scientist and UC Berkeley educated mathematition to ease the lives of people with color blindess through innovative lense technology.

Its technology filters out wavelengths of light that are primary causes of color deficiency. The M and L cones are altered to maximize the color differentiation, eliminating the "confusion line" of a color blind individual.

The lens technology increases contrast between the red and green color signals, which reduces the effects of color deficiency. Thus, color blinds can take a step closer to experiencing our world the way we do.
